About Us

Who We Are?

STABLU Sci-Tech has officially named on 1st January 2019. STABLU stand for STANDARD(ST) + ABILITY(AB) + LUXURY (LU) these are called 3 core concepts of STABLU-er, and it's extension Sci-Tech comes from the words Science and Technology.

Why We Do Exist?

Our main mission statement is "Create Innovative Solutions to make life better" with this mission in mind, STABLU-er always focuses on solutions that are possible. STABLU's cultures are keeping innovate, data-driven, and customer center. As a result, we are not just working but solving real-life problems. STABLU has one simple goal to make this world to be a better and more optimistic place, particularly for Cambodia. STABLU-er always believes in the power of willingness, hard-working (smartly), and the commitment to create a small positive change for the whole community.

What We Are Doing?

STABLU Sci-Tech is a cross functional team both engineering skills and social skills. However, STABLU is working on two main projects called High Tech Product(HTP) and Low Tech Product(LTP). The HTP is concentrating on more sophisticated problems and needs higher technology solutions. Currently, STABLU has been developing IoT Solutions for applying in Energy Sector, to reduce the initial cost, time, and maximize efficiency and energy-saving. Our goal is to provide simple but effective solutions for enabling the possibility of clean and renewable energy for everyone. Meanwhile, STABLU is also working on developing LTP solutions for solving the real-life problem which does not require high technology. LTP project has one main objective is to enable and open a window, the opportunity for technology enthusiasts, makers, creators, innovators, and DIY-er building or creating the solutions for real-life problems and for people who need them.
